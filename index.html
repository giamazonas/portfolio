Robota

Mobile-first Web App

Robota is an app that connects Ukrainian Refugees in the Washington state area with similarly located job posters. Seekers can filter the job by location, name, or by industry and can contact the poster via e-mail or a link directly to their site. Job posters can post a job directly through the app using the job poster page that will be visible to the seekers directly.

Implemented user sign in and styled mobile-first front-end of a decoupled app built via the MERN stack
